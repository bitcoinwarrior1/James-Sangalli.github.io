What learnings did you take from the chapter?

A: I am already familiar with the teachings laid out in the chapter as I have read books and watched videos that teach similar things. The teaching typically revolves around meditation and training the mind to be more calm. This is a skill like any other and it takes time to develop however its effects can be felt quick quickly when put into practise.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

A: I typically try to do 10 minutes of meditation each night so I know that this can be very relaxing but it can also be difficult at times as the mind has a tendency to want to think all the time. I found the 2 minute exercise to be calming but the advice of deep breathing during an emotional charged situation is also a great idea.
